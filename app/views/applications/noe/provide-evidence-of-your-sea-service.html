Seagoing service

Provide evidence of your sea service

You must submit two different forms of evidence supporting your declared service. This is usually Discharge Book entries and sea service testimonials.

If your application requires confirmation of watchkeeping, at least one form of evidence must support this.

If you do not have a Discharge Book, Certificates of Discharge can be provided instead. Alternative forms of evidence of service can also include a company letter.

If you have been working on Yachts, you may provide a Service Record Book from an approved organisation. If you are providing this you do not need to also submit a second form of evidence of your service.
