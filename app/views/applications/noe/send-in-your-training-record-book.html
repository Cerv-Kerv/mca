Identity and medical

Send your Training Record Book (TRB) to the Maritime and Coast Guard Agency (MCA)

Your training record book should list all performed activities, signed off by officers whilst at sea. All recorded training acts as evidence that will support your application.

Ensure your training book is complete. An incomplete training book can delay the procesing of your application or mean that it's not approved.

Sending in your Training Record Book

Add your Seafarer Reference Number to a piece of paper and attach it to your TRB before posting to us. We will pair it with the correct application you have submitted.

Your original, hard copy Training Record Book (TRB) must be sent to our office. You cannot upload this document.
