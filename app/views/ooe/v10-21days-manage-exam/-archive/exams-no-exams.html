Why can't I book an exam?

We cannot show any exams for you to book right now. This may be because you:

Need to arrange a resit

If you:

  • failed your last exam or,
  • cancelled within 21 days of the booked date

you will need to apply for a resit.

Check your inbox for your result or cancellation confirmation email. It will have instuctions on how you can apply and pay for a resit.

Need to do the Part A exam

Some MCA qualifications require passing the online proctored Part A exam before the Part B oral exam can be booked.

If you need to take the Part A exam you would have recieved instuctions with login details in your NOE email.

When you have passed Part A you will be sent a link to book your Part B oral exam. You must pass the Part B oral exam within 1 year of passing Part A.

Have an expired NOE

An NOE is valid for 5 years. If yours has expired, you need to get a new NOE by reapplying for a certificate of competency before you can book an exam.
