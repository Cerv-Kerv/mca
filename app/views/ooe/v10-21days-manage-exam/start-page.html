Book and manage an oral exam

Use this service to:

  • book an oral exam
  • change your exam date
  • cancel your exam

Booking an oral exam takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You will need your:

  • date of birth
  • Seafarer Documentation System (SDS) number

You can find SDS number in the email about your NOE (Notice of Eligibility).

You will need to have a valid NOE. NOE's are valid for 5 years from the date of issue.

If you do not have a valid NOE you will have to submit the required documents and application form.

Who can use this service

This service is for seafarers who are ready to take an oral examination towards obtaining a Certificate of Competency.

This includes:

  • Officers
  • Masters
  • Engineers
  • Cadets

If you are a Deck Officer

To book an oral exam for:

  • Officer of the Watch (OOW) unlimited or,
  • Master (under 200GT) / Officer of the Watch (yachts under 500GT)

You need to have passed the Part A: online computer-based proctored examination within the last 12 months.
