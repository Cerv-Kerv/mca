Seagoing service

Provide evidence of your sea service

You must submit two different types of evidence that support your declared service. These evidence-types will usually be your Discharge Book entries and sea service testimonials.

If your application requires confirmation of watchkeeping, at least one type of your provided evidence must support this.

If you do not have a Discharge Book, you can provide Certificates of Discharge instead. Alternative forms of evidence of service can also include a company letter.

If you have been working on Yachts, you may provide a Service Record Book from an approved organisation. If you are providing this you do not need to also submit a second type of evidence of your service.
