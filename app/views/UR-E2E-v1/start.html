Before you start

This prototype will show you one specific journey. Not all links or options within the prototype will work.

We will not ask you to complete everything that you would need to in a real-world application.

You will view a journey:

  • for a Deck Officer of the Watch (OOW) – Unlimited certificate,
  • for a foundation degree training route
  • using the online payment method
  • for someone that holds a UK Global Maritime Distress and Safety System (GMDSS)

Entering information

You can:

  • enter anything you choose into the fields that you see
  • feel reassured that you will not be blocked based on what you enter
  • feel reassured that nothing you enter will be saved

Payments

When entering payments:

  • the payment related screens are for testing purposes only
  • no payment will be taken

Knowing when you have finished

You will know you have finished when you reach the overview page that shows ‘In progress’ next to your CoC application.

After you finish

Answering questions about your experience will help us to improve the service.

Answer the questions to tell us what you found clear, confusing, easy, or frustrating.
