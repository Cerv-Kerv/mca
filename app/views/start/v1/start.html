What you will need

  • Seafarer Reference Number (SRN) to access the service
  • Correct amount of qualifying seagoing evidence
  • Evidence to support your application

Application costs

Application costs vary, depending on what you are applying for.

A new application costs £220. Applications for reapplying or upgrading, cost less, and the exact amount will be presented to you as you move through the pages within this service.

Before you start

Get help with your SRN number

If you do not have a SRN, you will need to request one. You will need your passport when requesting an SRN.

Registering for a SRN takes around 5 minutes.

Your SRN may also be known to you as a Seafarer Documentation System (SDS) number.

If you cannot find your SRN number, email:

  • deck@mcga.gov.uk

You can also apply by post.
