What you will need

To access this service, you will need:

  • Seafarer Reference Number (SRN)
  • Relevant evidence to support your application

If you do not have an SRN, you will need to request one. You will need your passport when requesting an SRN.

Registering for an SRN takes around 5 minutes.

Your SRN may also be known to you as a seafarer documentation system (SDS) number.
