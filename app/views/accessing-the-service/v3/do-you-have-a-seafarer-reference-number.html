Do you have a Seafarer Reference Number (SRN)?

Your seafarer reference number may also be known to you as a seafarer documentation system (SDS) number. This reference number is usually found in your Notice of Eligibility (NOE) email or other related documentation.

If you know that you have a seafarer reference number, but do not know what it is, select 'No'.

We will match your credentials to your existing Maritime and Coastal Agency (MCA) record.
