Your exam (Part A)

You must pass the online exam (Part A) before you can take Part B.

As long as your Notice of Eligibility (NoE) is and remains valid, you can take your exam (Part A) at any time.

Your exam (Part B) must be taken within 12 months of passing Part A. We'll send you information about booking Part B of your exam when you pass Part A.

Preparing for your exam

Before you take your exam, read the Candidate's code of conduct (opens in a new window) .
