Pass Part A exam

You must pass the Part A online computer-based exam before you can take the Part B oral exam.

You can take the exam at any time while your NOE is still valid.

After you pass Part A, you have 12 months to take your Part B oral exam. We'll send you information about booking Part B when you pass Part A.

Before you take the exam

You will need a username and password to access the exam. Check your email for this information.

Read the Candidate's code of conduct (opens in a new window). It tells you:

  • stationary required
  • what computer equipment you need
  • exam rules you must follow
