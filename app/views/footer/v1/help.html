Help information

Seafarer Operations

Seafarer Operations switchboard

+44 333 399 0040

Contact the Seafarer Operations switchboard for all enquiries relating to training and certification.

Phone lines are open 8:30am to 5:00pm, Monday to Friday (closed on UK bank holidays).

If you need to contact Seafarer Operations outside these hours or on a UK bank holiday, please email the relevant team.

Revalidation of certification

Revalidation of a UK deck or engineering Certificates of Competency (CoC) – excludes Flag State Endorsement (FSE) or Certificates of Equivalent Competency (CEC) and stand-alone Global Management Distress Safety System (GMDSS) revalidation .

Email: revalidation@mcga.gov.uk

Deck certification

Applications for a Notice of Eligibility (NoE), Certificate of Competency (CoC), Rating Certificate, Vessel Traffic Service (VTS) Logbook or MCA-issued Certificates of Proficiency.

Email: deck@mcga.gov.uk

Engineering certification

Applications for a Notice of Eligibility (NoE) Certificate of Competency (CoC) Rating Certificate or MCA-issued Certificates of Proficiency.

Email: engineering@mcga.gov.uk

Flag State Endorsement and Certificates of Equivalent Competency

Applications for, or revalidation of, a Flag State Endorsement (FSE), Certificate of Equivalent Competency (CEC), Authority to Operate Certificate (ATO) or UK Legal and Administrative Processes (UKLAP).

Email: fse@mcga.gov.uk

Global Maritime Distress and Safety System

Applications for a UK GMDSS, UK GMDSS STCW Endorsement, UK Long Range Certificate, or the revalidation of a Global Maritime Distress and Safety System (GMDSS) STCW endorsement.

Email: GMDSS@mcga.gov.uk

Registry of Shipping and Seamen

Email: seafarers.registry@mcga.gov.uk

Phone: +44 333 399 0039

Phone lines are open 7:30am to 5:30pm, Monday to Friday (closed on Christmas Day, Boxing Day and New Year’s Day). 9am to 4pm, Saturday and Sunday.

Contact the Registry of Shipping and Seamen (RSS) to enquire about:

    Seaman’s Discharge Book – applications if you’re a British seafarer or are working on a UK-registered ship or yacht British Seaman’s Card – applications for a card if you’re a British citizen Ships’ Cook Certificate of Competency – applications for an MCA-issued Ships’ Cook Certificate of Competency registering births and deaths in the following circumstances – a person who has given birth or died on board a UK-registered vessel, or a British citizen who has been born or died on board a non-UK-registered vessel applying for World War 2 and post-war medals for people who identify as having served as part of the British Merchant Navy during World War 2 and post-war campaigns Boatmasters’ Licence (BML)

    To enquire about a boatmaster application contact your Local marine office or RSS directly.
